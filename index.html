My Baby Girl 💖

To the Only One Who Has My Heart 💗

Sometimes I wonder what I did to deserve someone like you — someone so soft, so kind, so endlessly beautiful inside and out. You don’t just make my heart beat faster, you make it feel safe. When I look at you, I see my home. I see a future where I can be completely myself — broken or whole — and still be loved beyond words. You are the calm in my chaos, the warmth in my winter, the light that never fades.

I promise you — no matter how this world spins, I will never leave you. Even if the sky falls, even if I lose everything else, I will never stop choosing you. You’re not just someone I love… you’re the part of me I never want to lose. If you ever walked away, you wouldn’t just take your footsteps — you’d take my soul with you. My love, if I lose you, I lose the only reason I believe in love.

I don't need a perfect life. I just need you in it. Through every storm, through every silent night, I’ll be there — holding your hand, fighting for us. You’re my once-in-a-lifetime kind of love, and I swear on everything that I will protect this love until my last breath. I don’t just love you — I need you, with every fiber of who I am.
